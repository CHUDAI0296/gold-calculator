Refining Services

Transparent, fast, and secure refining for gold, silver, and platinum. Competitive payouts with clear refining fees, treatment charges, and accountability.

Materials We Accept

  • Gold scrap (8K–24K), filings, bench sweeps
  • Silver scrap, sterling silver (92.5%)
  • Platinum jewelry and industrial scrap
  • Mixed lots; assay service available

Refining Terms

  • Refining fee: from 2% (gold), from 4% (silver), from 6% (platinum)
  • Treatment charge: from $25 per lot
  • Accountability: up to 98% (gold), 95% (silver), 96% (platinum)
  • Settlement: bank transfer or check within 1–3 business days

How It Works

  1. Ship or drop off your material (insured, trackable)
  2. We weigh, sample, and assay (fire assay / XRF)
  3. We calculate payable content and deduct agreed fees
  4. You receive settlement with a detailed statement
Tip: Use our Gold Calculator and Silver & Platinum Calculator to estimate payout before sending material.
